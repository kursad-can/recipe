Kursad's Omlette
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 tablespoon milk
Salt and pepper
1 tablespoon butter
Optional: chopped vegetables, grated cheese, herbs

Instructions:
Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
Pour egg mixture into the skillet.
Cook until edges set, about 2 minutes.
Add optional ingredients to one side.
Fold the omelette in half.
Cook 1-2 more minutes.
Serve immediately.
